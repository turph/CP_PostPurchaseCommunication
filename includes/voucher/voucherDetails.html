Where to Exchange Your Voucher

Please print a single copy of this CityPASS voucher before arriving at the first attraction you visit. Present your voucher on or before [month day year] at any one of the following locations to redeem it for your CityPASS booklets:

  • Space Needle: Seattle Center; ticket booth or will call
  • Seattle Aquarium: Pier 59, Seattle Central Waterfront; member/prepaid ticket window
  • Argosy Cruises Harbor Tour: Pier 55, Seattle Central Waterfront; Argosy Pier 56 visitor center or Pier 55 ticket booth (Note: Please arrive at least 30 minutes prior to planned departure time.)
  • EMP Museum*: 325 5th Ave. North; main admissions desk
  • Woodland Park Zoo*: North 50th St. & Fremont Ave. North; any Woodland Park Zoo entry gate ticket window (excluding the membership window)
  • Pacific Science Center*: Seattle Center; general admission entrance, excluding member or express entrances
  • Chihuly Garden And Glass*: Seattle Center; admissions counter located in the main entrance lobby

Groups of 10 or More: To avoid delays, some attractions have specific arrival instructions for large groups. Please visit citypass.com/groups for more information.

How To Use Your E-ticket

Please print this e-ticket before arriving at the first park you visit and fill in the date of first use (below). Present the e-ticket for entry to each park. You can visit the parks in any order you wish.

The first usage of this e-ticket must occur on or after January 1, 2017. This e-ticket is valid for 14 days including first day of use; it expires 13 days after first use or January 13, 2018, whichever occurs first.

Where to Present This E-ticket

Disneyland Resort and Disney California Adventure Park - 1313 S. Harbor Blvd, Anaheim, CA 92802

  • Present printed CityPASS e-ticket at turnstile to receive your 3-Day Disneyland Resort Park Hopper® Ticket, good for any three days during the 14-day validity period. Please b prepared to show photo ID for admission. (Keep your printed e-ticket for admission to the other parks.)
  • Includes 1 Magic Morning.*
  • Open 365 days; hours vary daily. Call (714) 781-4565 to confirm hours, or visit www.disneyland.com for possible attraction closures.

SeaWorld San Diego - 500 Sea World Drive, San Diego, CA 92109

  • Present printed CityPASS e-ticket at turnstile to skip the main ticket line and receive your one day general admission to the world-famous aquatic theme park, including all rides and shows.
  • Open 365 days; hours vary daily. Call (800) 257-4268 or visit www.seaworldsandiego.com to confirm hours.

LEGOLAND California - One LEGOLAND Drive, Carlsbad, CA 92008

  • Present printed CityPASS e-ticket at turnstile for your one-day general admission to LEGOLAND California Park.**
  • Open daily during spring break, summer and select holiday periods; open Thursday - Monday the rest of the year. Hours vary daily. Visit LEGOLAND.com for days and hours of operation.
